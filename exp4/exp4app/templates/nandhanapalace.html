Nandhana Palace

Nandhana Palace, a renowned name in the culinary world for authentic Andhra cuisine, has a prominent branch in Chromepet, Chennai, offering a rich and flavorful dining experience. The restaurant is strategically located at No. 322, 1st Floor, Hemraj Palace, Mit Flyover, GST Road, making it easily accessible for both locals and visitors. The establishment provides a fine-dining experience with a cozy yet refined ambiance, warm lighting, and tasteful decor that is suitable for various occasions, including family dinners, dates, and business lunches. The menu at Nandhana Palace is a delightful blend of traditional Andhra, North Indian, and Chinese cuisines, ensuring a diverse range of options for all palates. Their acclaimed biryanis, such as the Supreme Boneless Chicken Biryani and Nellore Mutton Biryani, are highly popular and considered must-try dishes, served with traditional accompaniments like raita and salan. Beyond biryanis, patrons rave about signature starters like Bamboo Chicken, Mutton Pepper Fry, and Carrot 65, which are known for their authentic spices and robust flavors. The restaurant also offers wholesome Andhra veg meals and unique desserts like the Elaneer Payasam (tender coconut pudding).Service at the Chromepet branch consistently receives high praise from customers, with staff noted for their fast, attentive, and hospitable approach. The restaurant is well-equipped with modern amenities, including air conditioning, clean restrooms (including wheelchair-accessible options), and ample parking space for vehicles. Nandhana Palace offers dine-in, takeaway, and home delivery services, providing flexibility for its clientele. Overall, it stands as a celebrated destination for those seeking the "true taste of Andhra" in Chennai, dedicated to quality ingredients and exceptional hospitality.
