Copper Kitchen

Copper Kitchen in Chromepet, Chennai is a popular and well-regarded non-vegetarian restaurant known for its diverse and flavorful South and North Indian cuisines, including Chettinad, Tandoor, Andhra, and unique Tamil Muslim wedding Biryani dishes. Established in 2011, the restaurant chain emphasizes quality, hygiene, and excellent hospitality, aiming to provide a memorable dining experience in a warm, contemporary ambiance. The restaurant is conveniently located at 406, GST Road, near Chennai Silks, a prominent location in the bustling Chromepet area, making it easily accessible via various modes of transport. It offers a casual, family-friendly atmosphere with both indoor seating and options for home delivery and takeout. The staff is often noted for being attentive and friendly, contributing positively to the overall dining experience. The establishment also provides practical amenities like free Wi-Fi and ample parking for both two- and four-wheelers. The extensive menu is a major draw, featuring an array of dishes that cater to different palates. Signature and highly recommended items include the traditional Mutton Soup, the spicy and tender Dynamite Chicken, and the much-praised Chicken 65 Biryani. Other popular items range from various BBQ and Tandoor specialties like Basa Fish Tawa Grill and Murg Malai Kebab to South Indian staples such as Chettinad Prawn Thokku and the innovative Special Kizhi Parotta. For dessert, options like the Tender Coconut Pudding and Brownie with Ice-cream are popular choices. The restaurant is also known for introducing large-format items like the full goat biriyani, a unique attraction for group dining.Beyond its restaurant services, the Chromepet branch features a large, pillarless banquet hall that can seat up to 350 guests for events like weddings and corporate gatherings. This venue is well-equipped with modern luxury amenities, including air conditioning, power backup, and dedicated dining areas, making it a sought-after event destination in the area. Overall, Copper Kitchen is praised for its rich, flavorful dishes and good service, though some minor concerns about portion sizes or service during peak hours have been occasionally noted in reviews. It remains a top choice for those seeking an authentic and diverse non-vegetarian meal in Chennai.
