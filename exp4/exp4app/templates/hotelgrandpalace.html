Hotel Grand Palace

Located in the vibrant and commercially active neighborhood of Chromepet in Southern Chennai, Hotel Grand Palace stands as a prominent and established accommodation choice. This 3-star property is strategically situated on the Grand Southern Trunk (GST) Road, making it an incredibly convenient base for travelers. Its proximity to key transport hubs is a major advantage: the Chennai International Airport is a mere 6 kilometers away, while the Chromepet railway station is within a comfortable 10-minute walk. This accessibility appeals greatly to both business travelers attending meetings in the area and tourists exploring the region. The hotel features a classically designed facade and an interior that balances traditional elegance with modern functionality. It offers 36 well-appointed, air-conditioned guest rooms. In an effort to consistently meet guest expectations, the hotel has undertaken recent renovations, ensuring that rooms are clean, well-maintained, and up-to-date. The decor often incorporates formal elements like marble flooring and traditional wooden furnishings, creating a sophisticated yet comfortable environment. Each room is equipped with amenities designed for a seamless stay, including complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi access, flat-screen televisions, mini-bars, and 24-hour room service. Guest safety and convenience are prioritized with measures such as CCTV surveillance on all floors and efficient valet parking services.Hotel Grand Palace provides a variety of dining experiences within its premises. Guests can start their day with a complimentary buffet breakfast. For other meals, there is an international restaurant serving a range of cuisines, a lively sports bar for relaxation, and a pleasant rooftop dining area that offers an alternative ambiance. The hotel is also a versatile venue for corporate and social events. It boasts a dedicated business center, a conference room for meetings, and an ornate banquet hall capable of hosting functions ranging from business seminars to intimate weddings and receptions. Feedback from guests typically highlights the hotel's attentive and helpful staff, the cleanliness of the facilities, and its excellent value for money. The blend of a convenient location, well-maintained rooms, diverse amenities, and reliable service solidifies Hotel Grand Palace's reputation as a reliable and popular accommodation option in the Chromepet area of Chennai.
